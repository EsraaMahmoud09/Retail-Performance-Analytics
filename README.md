# Retail-Performance-Analytics

##  Overview

This project presents a **Sales Performance Dashboard** built using Excel, Power Query, and DAX to analyze business data and generate actionable insights.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze sales and profitability
* Identify high & low-performing products
* Understand customer and shipping behavior
* Detect seasonal trends
* Provide business recommendations

---

##  Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Power Query
* DAX

---

##  Dataset Includes

* Sales Transactions
* Profit & Discounts
* Product Categories
* Shipping Modes
* Geographic Data
* Monthly Trends

---


##  Key Insights

###  Financial Performance

* **Total Sales:** $59,491
* **Total Profit:** $2,790
* **Profit Margin:** 4.7% 

 Low margin indicates high costs or heavy discounting

---

###  Category Performance

* **Office Supplies:** Highest demand but lower profitability
* **Furniture:** Low margins due to shipping costs
* **Technology:** High-value products

---

###  Geographic Insights

* **Top State:** New York
* **Weak States:** Maryland, Rhode Island

 Opportunity for market expansion

---

###  Shipping Analysis

* **Most Used:** Standard Class
* **Most Profitable:** First Class

 Faster shipping generates higher profit

---

###  Seasonality

* **Peak:** December
* **Low:** March

 Sales vary significantly across months

---

##  Recommendations

###  Improve Profitability

* Reduce excessive discounts
* Optimize pricing strategy

---

###  Focus on High-Margin Products

* Promote Accessories & Fasteners
* Apply slight price adjustments

---

###  Geographic Expansion

* Retain customers in New York
* Improve performance in Pennsylvania & Ohio

---

###  Shipping Optimization

* Promote First Class shipping
* Adjust Standard Class pricing

---

###  Inventory Planning

* Increase stock before Q4
* Reduce stock in Q1
